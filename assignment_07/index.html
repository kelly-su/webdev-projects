Final Project Proposal: Personal Website/Digital Portfolio

Topic

This personal website will serve as a digital portfolio, showcasing my academic projects, interests in web development, and product management.

Audience

The intended audience includes potential employers, collaborators, and fellow students interested in computer science or product management.

Design & Style

The website will have a clean, modern aesthetic that emphasizes readability and user-friendly navigation. The design will feature a minimalistic look with pastel colors to make the content visually engaging.

Color Palette

  • Primary: Soft Blue (#3a7ca5)
  • Secondary: Warm Beige (#f5f1e3)
  • Accent: Deep Navy (#1d3557)
  • Background: Light Grey (#f9f9f9)

Inspiration Websites

  • Carrd.co
  • Pinterest
  • Webflow

Scope

Pages and Content

  1. About Me Page
    • Details on academic background, skills, and a brief career overview.
    • Sections: Background, Interests (web development, product management).
  2. Projects Page
    • Overview of key projects with descriptions, tech stack, and links.
    • Sections: Featured Projects, Additional Projects (grid format).
  3. Resume Page
    • Interactive resume with key sections like Education, Experience, Skills, and a download link.
    • Sections: Education and Experience, Download Button.
  4. Contact Page
    • Contact form and links to LinkedIn, GitHub, and email.
