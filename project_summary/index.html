Project Summary

Topic and Purpose

This project is a personal portfolio website designed to showcase my academic background, technical skills, and aspirations in the fields of computer science and product management. The purpose of the site is to provide a professional and engaging platform for potential employers, collaborators, and peers to explore my work and connect with me.

Implemented Features

  • Responsive Design: The website adapts to different screen sizes, ensuring optimal viewing on mobile, tablet, and desktop devices.
  • Contact Form: Visitors can easily reach out via a functional contact form that redirects to a confirmation page upon submission.
  • Interactive Project Showcase: A visually appealing Projects page highlights key projects with animations and a demonstration video for one project.
  • HTML5 Media Integration: The site includes a video element to provide a dynamic user experience.
  • Transitions & Transformations: Hover effects and animations enhance interactivity across the site.

Technologies Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript: The foundation for the structure, design, and interactivity of the site.
